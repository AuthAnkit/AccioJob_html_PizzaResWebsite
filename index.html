MENU

Beyond the classics, the menu itself is thoughtfully curated. It offers a brilliant range of options, from daring seasonal specials and gourmet white pizzas to hearty pasta dishes and fresh, vibrant salads.

New-York Style

Large, wide slices often sold individually. The crust is thin and flexible enough to be folded in half to eat. It has a crispy edge and a chewy interior.

Californian Style

Known for thin crusts and use of unconventional, gourmet, or fusion toppings

DeepDish Pizza

Baked in a deep, circular pan, giving it a high edge perfect for layers of cheese, toppings, and sauce.

Neapolitan Pizza

Thin, soft, and elastic center with a high, puffy, and slightly charred edge

Sicilian Pizza

Thick-crusted, square, or rectangular pizza. The dough is spongy and bread-like. Often topped with onions

Detroit Style

Rectangular pizza with a thick, chewy, airy crust. It is traditionally baked in square, blue steel pans
